Info About Mymensingh

Mymensingh is a metropolitan city and capital of Mymensingh Division, Bangladesh. Located on the bank of Brahmaputra River, about 120 km north of the national capital Dhaka, it is a major financial center and educational hub of north-central Bangladesh.
